# Home Eye Test Service

## Eligibility
- Available in 40+ Indian cities (metros + most tier-2)
- Free for Gold members (1 test per quarter)
- Rs 149 for non-members, refundable as store credit on any purchase over Rs 2,000 within 30 days

## Booking
- Via Lenskart app → Services → Book Home Eye Test
- Slot confirmation within 15 minutes via SMS + WhatsApp
- Standard slots: 10 AM – 8 PM, 7 days a week

## What to Expect
- Certified optometrist visits with portable autorefractor + trial lens kit
- 20–30 minute full eye check-up
- Prescription shared on-spot via app + printed copy
- Frame try-on (optometrist brings 40+ frames)

## Cancellation by Customer
- Free cancellation up to 2 hours before slot
- Within 2 hours: Rs 100 deducted (non-Gold); free for Gold

## Cancellation by Optometrist
- If Lenskart cancels/reschedules: full refund + Rs 200 apology credit
- If 2 consecutive cancellations: complimentary premium frame (up to Rs 3,000) OR store visit reimbursement
- Escalate to regional ops manager after 2 cancellations

## Post-Test
- Prescription valid for 6 months for purchases
- Free one-time Rx adjustment within 15 days if customer reports discomfort
