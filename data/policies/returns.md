# Lenskart Return Policy

## Eyewear Returns (Eyeglasses, Sunglasses)
- Return window: **14 days** from delivery date
- Frame must be unused, with all original packaging and tags
- Prescription lenses are non-returnable unless defective
- Zero-power / plano lenses are fully returnable
- Refund processed within **5–7 business days** to original payment method

## Contact Lens Returns
- Unopened boxes only, within **7 days** of delivery
- Opened boxes cannot be returned (hygiene policy)
- Exception: manufacturing defect — full replacement within 30 days

## Defective Product
- Report within 48 hours with photo/video evidence
- Free pickup + 100% refund OR replacement at customer's choice
- Escalate to Quality Team if issue persists

## Non-Returnable Items
- Custom prescription lenses (unless defective)
- Used contact lenses
- Gift cards and accessories bundled with offers

## How Customer Initiates
1. Open Lenskart app → My Orders → Return
2. Select reason from dropdown
3. Pickup scheduled within 48 hours
4. Refund initiated after QC at warehouse
