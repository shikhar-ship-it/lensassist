# Order Status & Delivery

## Standard Delivery Timelines
- **Metro cities**: 3–5 business days
- **Tier 2/3 cities**: 5–8 business days
- **International (UAE, SG, TH)**: 7–14 business days
- Prescription orders add 2–3 days for lens fitting

## Tracking
- Tracking link sent via SMS + email once shipped
- Real-time status on Lenskart app → My Orders
- Courier partners: Delhivery, BlueDart, DTDC, Ekart

## Delayed Order Policy
- If delay exceeds promised EDD by 3+ days, customer eligible for:
  - Rs 100 credit to Lenskart wallet
  - OR free upgrade to premium packaging on next order
- Auto-triggered by ops once SLA breach detected

## Order Modification
- Address change: allowed within 2 hours of order placement
- Prescription change: allowed before lens fitting begins (typically 6 hrs)
- Cancellation: free before dispatch; Rs 150 restocking after dispatch

## Failed Delivery
- 2 re-attempts automatic
- 3rd attempt requires customer confirmation via WhatsApp
- After 5 days of RTO attempts, order cancelled + full refund
