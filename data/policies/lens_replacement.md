# Lens Replacement Policy

## Power Change (Rx Update)
- First lens redo **FREE** within 6 months of purchase if prescription changed
- Requires updated prescription from certified optometrist
- Available at any Lenskart store or via home pickup

## Wrong Power Dispensed
- 100% free replacement + apology credit of Rs 200
- Raise ticket within 30 days with Rx copy
- Priority processing: lens fitted within 5 days

## Lens Upgrade
- Pay the difference between old and new lens type
- Example: Standard AR → Blu-Cut AR = Rs 800 upgrade charge
- Process: drop frame at store, pick up in 3–5 days

## Progressive/Bifocal Adaptation
- 30-day adaptation guarantee: if customer cannot adapt, switch to single-vision at no cost
- One-time adjustment per order

## Contact Lens Rx Change
- Unopened boxes exchanged for correct power within 30 days
- Must present valid Rx from certified optometrist
