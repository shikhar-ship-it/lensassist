# Payment, EMI & Refunds

## Payment Methods
- UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, COD, Lenskart wallet, gift cards
- EMI partners: Bajaj Finserv, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, SBI, ZestMoney, Simpl
- EMI tenure: 3, 6, 9, 12 months. No-cost EMI on select orders over Rs 3,500

## Refund Processing Timelines
- Prepaid orders: refund initiated within 48 hours of QC approval
  - UPI: 2–3 business days
  - Credit/debit card: 5–7 business days
  - Net banking: 3–5 business days
- COD orders: refund via UPI/bank transfer only; customer must share account details in app

## Stuck Refund (>7 business days)
- Customer can file trace request via app → Orders → Refund Status
- Ops team provides bank reference number (RRN) within 24 hours
- If RRN is valid but amount not credited, customer's bank is responsible — share the RRN with the bank

## EMI Issues
- Duplicate EMI deduction: auto-reversed within 5 working days
- If bank doesn't reverse, Lenskart credits the amount to wallet as goodwill within 3 days
- EMI plan cancellation only possible before dispatch

## Price Match / Price Drop Protection
- No automatic price match after purchase
- Discretionary credit: if price drops within 7 days of purchase by >15%, one-time Rs credit to wallet (max Rs 500) at supervisor discretion
- Not applicable on flash sales, bank offers, or limited-time coupons

## Cancellation Fees
- Before dispatch: free, full refund to original method
- After dispatch, before delivery: Rs 150 restocking fee deducted
- After delivery (used as return): standard 14-day return policy applies
