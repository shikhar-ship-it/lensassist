# Lenskart Warranty Policy

## Frame Warranty
- **1 year** manufacturing warranty on all frames
- Covers: hinge breakage, screw loosening, coating defects, frame discoloration
- Does NOT cover: accidental damage, scratches from normal use, sat-on frames

## Lens Warranty
- **6 months** on anti-glare, blue-cut, photochromic coatings
- Covers: peeling, delamination, coating crack
- Does NOT cover: scratches from improper cleaning, deep impact cracks

## Gold Membership (Extended)
- Gold members get **2 year** frame warranty
- 1-time free lens replacement within 6 months of purchase if prescription changes

## How to Claim
1. Visit nearest Lenskart store OR raise ticket via app
2. Bring/upload original invoice + product
3. QC team assesses defect within 3 days
4. Replacement/repair within 7–10 days

## Exclusions
- Frames sold under clearance sale (marked "as-is")
- Third-party lenses fitted after purchase
- Damage from swimming pool chlorine or seawater exposure
