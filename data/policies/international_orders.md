# International Orders (UAE · Saudi Arabia · Singapore · Thailand)

## Delivery Timelines
- UAE / Saudi Arabia: 5–9 business days
- Singapore: 7–10 business days
- Thailand: 8–14 business days
- Add 2–3 days for prescription lens fitting in India before shipping

## Customs & Duties
- Duty included in price for UAE and Singapore orders (no surprise charges)
- Saudi Arabia: customer pays 5% VAT at delivery (standard KSA rule)
- Thailand: customer pays duty based on declared value; Lenskart provides proforma invoice

## Stuck at Customs
- If tracking shows "pending customs clearance" for >3 business days:
  - Ops initiates dispute with courier
  - Lenskart provides customs paperwork directly to customer if requested
  - If package is held >7 days, full refund + reshipment at no cost

## Returns (International)
- 14-day return window from delivery
- Customer pays return shipping; Lenskart reimburses on successful refund
- Frames only — prescription lenses non-returnable internationally (fit issues handled via lens replacement, not full return)

## Warranty Claims Abroad
- Ship to nearest Lenskart hub (India for SAARC, UAE for GCC, Singapore for SEA)
- Free return shipping on valid warranty claims
- Local Lenskart stores (if available) can accept warranty returns directly:
  - UAE: Dubai Mall, Mall of Emirates, Ibn Battuta
  - Singapore: Jewel Changi, Plaza Singapura
  - Thailand: currently online-only, no physical stores

## Currency
- Pricing shown in AED / SAR / SGD / THB based on shipping country
- Refunds in original purchase currency
