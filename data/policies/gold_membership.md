# Lenskart Gold Membership

## Benefits
- Flat 50% off on second frame (any purchase)
- Free shipping on all orders
- Free eye test at home (1 per quarter)
- 2-year frame warranty (vs 1 year standard)
- Priority customer support (avg response < 5 min)
- Early access to new launches and sales

## Pricing
- Annual: Rs 600
- 2-year: Rs 999 (save Rs 201)

## How to Activate
- Sign up via app → Profile → Gold Membership
- Auto-renewal unless cancelled 7 days before expiry

## Cancellation / Refund
- Full refund within 7 days of purchase if no benefits availed
- Pro-rated refund if cancelled within 30 days (minus benefits used)
- No refund after 30 days

## Sharing
- Gold benefits are non-transferable, tied to registered mobile number
- Family add-on available at Rs 200 per member (up to 4)
